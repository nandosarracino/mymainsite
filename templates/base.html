About me!

Yá’át’ééh, shi ei Fernando Sarracino yinishye. Kinyaa’aanii nishli doo Ma’ii Deeshgiizhinii bashishchiin. Naakaii Dine’e dashichei doo Tabaaha dashinali. Niʼiijíhí ei dah shi neesaah.

Hello, my name is Fernando Sarracino. I am Towering House clan born for the Coyote Pass Clan. My maternal grandparents are of the Mexican Clan and my paternal grandparents are of the Water Edge clan. I come from Navajo, NM.

I have received a Bachelor degree in Computer Science at New Mexico Highlands University and I am currently a Graduate Student at NMHU obtaining my Masters in Computer Science. My hobbies consist of playing guitar, working on vehicles and listening to music.
